Privacy Policy

How RE Report handles visitor information.

Introduction

RE Report is committed to protecting the privacy of visitors to rereport.org. This policy describes what information our site collects and how it is used.

Information Collection

RE Report is a static informational website. Our site does not use cookies, tracking pixels, or third-party analytics scripts. No personal information is collected through the browsing of this website.

If you contact our team via email, the information you provide (name, email address, message content) is used solely to respond to your inquiry. Contact information is not shared with third parties, sold, or used for marketing purposes.

Server Logs

Our hosting provider may automatically collect standard server log data, including IP addresses, browser type, referring pages, and access timestamps. This data is used solely for server administration and security monitoring. It is not used for tracking, profiling, or marketing.

Third-Party Links

RE Report does not link to external company websites from its ratings pages. Any references to company domains are provided as plain text for identification purposes only. RE Report is not responsible for the privacy practices of any external website.

Children's Privacy

RE Report does not knowingly collect information from individuals under the age of 13. This website is intended for adult consumers researching real estate services.

Changes to This Policy

RE Report may update this privacy policy periodically. Changes will be reflected on this page with a revised effective date.

Contact

For questions about this privacy policy, contact our team at info@rereport.org.
