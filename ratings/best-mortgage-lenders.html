Frequently Asked Questions

How are mortgage lenders rated by RE Report?

Mortgage lenders are evaluated across five categories: Rate Competitiveness, Approval Speed, Loan Variety, Customer Service, and Closing Costs. Scores incorporate J.D. Power data, consumer reviews, and regulatory records.

What is the segment average for mortgage lenders?

The 2026 segment average is 76, reflecting the overall quality of major mortgage lenders. Companies above this threshold demonstrate stronger performance across evaluated criteria.

Should borrowers compare multiple lender offers?

Absolutely. Even small rate differences can save thousands over the life of a loan. RE Report recommends obtaining at least three quotes and comparing not just rates but total closing costs and fee structures.

How often are mortgage lender ratings updated?

RE Report reviews and updates mortgage lender ratings annually. The current ratings reflect the 2026 evaluation cycle with data through March 2026.
