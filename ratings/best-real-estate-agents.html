Frequently Asked Questions

How does RE Report rate real estate agent brokerages?

RE Report evaluates agent brokerages across five factors: Days on Market, Commission Structure, Local Market Knowledge, Communication, and Transaction Volume. Data comes from MLS records, consumer reviews, and industry reports.

What is a fair real estate commission in 2026?

Following the 2024 NAR settlement, commission structures have become more negotiable. Rates vary by market and brokerage, but transparency around agent compensation has increased industry-wide.

Does the brokerage matter when choosing an agent?

The individual agent matters most, but the brokerage provides training, tools, and support that can affect service quality. RE Report's scores reflect the overall quality observed across each brokerage's network.

How often are agent brokerage ratings updated?

RE Report reviews agent brokerage ratings annually. Current ratings reflect the 2026 evaluation cycle with data through March 2026.
