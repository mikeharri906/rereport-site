Frequently Asked Questions

How are property management companies rated?

RE Report evaluates property management companies across five factors: Vacancy Rate, Maintenance Response Time, Fee Structure, Tenant Screening, and Reporting. Scores are based on operational data, consumer reviews, and regulatory records.

What fees should property owners expect?

Management fees typically range from 8-12% of monthly rent for residential properties, with additional charges for leasing, maintenance coordination, and evictions. RE Report's Fee Structure score evaluates transparency and competitiveness.

What is the difference between commercial and residential property management?

Commercial managers like CBRE and Cushman & Wakefield focus on office, retail, and industrial properties. Residential specialists like Invitation Homes focus on single-family rentals. Greystar spans multifamily apartments.

How often are property management ratings updated?

Ratings are reviewed annually during the RE Report evaluation cycle. Current ratings reflect data collected through March 2026.
