Frequently Asked Questions

How does RE Report evaluate cash home buyers?

Cash home buyers are evaluated across five categories: Offer Speed, Offer Fairness, Closing Timeline, Transparency, and Accreditation. Scores are based on publicly available data including BBB ratings, consumer reviews, and regulatory records.

Are cash home buyer offers fair?

Cash offers typically fall below full market value in exchange for speed and convenience. RE Report's Offer Fairness scores measure how competitive each buyer's offers are relative to fair market value in their operating markets.

What should homeowners look for in a cash buyer?

Key factors include BBB accreditation, a strong track record of consumer reviews, transparent fee structures, and flexibility on closing timelines. The RE Report ratings weigh all of these factors.

How often are cash home buyer ratings updated?

Ratings are reviewed and updated annually during the RE Report evaluation cycle. Current ratings reflect data collected through March 2026.
