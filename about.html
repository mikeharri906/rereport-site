About RE Report

Independent research. No advertising. No affiliates. No paid placements.

Who We Are

RE Report is an independent team of real estate consumers, investors, and researchers dedicated to bringing transparency to the real estate services industry. Our analysts evaluate companies across all major real estate categories, providing data-driven ratings that help consumers make informed decisions.

Our Mission

The real estate industry is filled with companies making bold claims about their services. Our mission is to cut through marketing language and provide objective, research-backed evaluations that consumers can trust. Every company rated by RE Report is evaluated using the same standardized methodology, regardless of company size or market presence.

What We Cover

Our team evaluates companies across eight major real estate categories:

  • Cash Land Buyers
  • Cash Home Buyers
  • iBuyer Companies
  • Mortgage Lenders
  • Title Companies
  • Real Estate Agents and Brokerages
  • Property Management Companies
  • Hard Money Lenders

Within each category, companies are scored on five category-specific factors using publicly available data. Scores are updated periodically as new data becomes available.

Our Independence

RE Report does not accept advertising revenue, affiliate commissions, or paid placements of any kind. Companies cannot pay for higher ratings or premium placement within our rankings. Our revenue model is entirely separate from the companies evaluated on this site.

No company has editorial input into its rating. Our analysts follow a consistent, documented methodology that is applied uniformly to every company evaluated. Detailed information about our scoring process is available on our Methodology page.
