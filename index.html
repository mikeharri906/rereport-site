How Our Ratings Work

RE Report evaluates companies using a proprietary methodology that synthesizes multiple public data sources. Every company is scored across five category-specific factors on a 100-point scale. Our team does not accept advertising, affiliate commissions, or paid placements of any kind. Companies cannot pay for a higher rating.
